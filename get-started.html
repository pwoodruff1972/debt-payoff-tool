Get Started Guide

Welcome! This guide will walk you through creating your personalized debt payoff plan in just a few simple steps.

Step 1: Add Your Debts

The first step is to list all of your current debts. Our tool is 100% private, so this information is only saved to your browser and never seen by us.

  1. Fill in the **Debt Name/Due Date**, **Current Balance**, **Interest Rate (APR)**, and **Minimum Monthly Payment**.
  2. Click the **"Add Debt"** button.
  3. Repeat this for all your debts. The free version allows up to 5 debts.

Step 2: Create Your Payoff Plan

Once your debts are listed, you're ready to create your plan.

  1. Choose your preferred strategy: **Debt Snowball** (for motivation) or **Debt Avalanche** (to save the most on interest). Both are free to use.
  2. Set your **Total Monthly Payment** budget. This is the total amount you can afford to put towards all your debts each month.
  3. Click the **"Calculate Plan"** button.

Your full, month-by-month payment schedule will instantly appear below!

Step 4: Unlock Advanced Insights with Pro

The Pro version unlocks powerful features for users who want to take their financial planning to the next level.

Visualize Your Journey with the Dashboard Pro

The Pro Dashboard gives you two powerful charts: a **Pie Chart** to see your debt breakdown and a **Line Chart** that projects your balance dropping to zero. It's the ultimate motivational tool.

Find the Fastest Path with the "What If" Scenario Planner Pro

Instantly see how your decisions impact your debt-free date. The "What If" planner lets you compare your original plan to scenarios where you add an extra monthly payment or make a one-time lump sum payment, showing you exactly how much time and money you'll save.
