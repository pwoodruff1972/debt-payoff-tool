Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 5, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Debt Payoff Planner application is designed to be a completely private, client-side tool.

What Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal or financial information. All data you enter into this tool—including your debt details, budget, and payment plans—is stored locally on your own computer within your web browser's local storage. We have no access to this information.

How Your Data is Used

Your data is used solely for the purpose of the application's functionality, which runs entirely in your browser. It is never sent to us or any third party.

License Keys

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is also stored locally in your browser's storage to unlock Pro features. We do not link this key to your personal identity.
