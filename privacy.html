Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 10, 2025

Your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy explains our principles regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of your information.

The Core Principle: We Don't Collect Your Data

The Debt Payoff Planner is a client-side application. This means that all the data you enter—including your debt names, balances, interest rates, and payment plans—is stored **only on your local device** within your web browser's storage. Your financial information is never sent to, stored on, or seen by our servers. We have no access to your data.

Information We Collect

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to help us understand how our visitors use the site and to improve our service. This data is aggregated and does not contain any personally identifiable information. We do not track you across other websites.

License Keys

If you purchase a Pro license, your license key is also stored in your browser's local storage to unlock pro features. We do not link this key to any personal information on our end.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us by email: support@privatedebtplanner.com
